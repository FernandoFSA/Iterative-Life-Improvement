# Appendix B — Evaluation Tools and Methods

## B.1 Diagnostic Methods
- MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) structure
- Brainstorming and hypothesis testing
- Root cause analysis (5 Whys, Ishikawa)

## B.2 Evaluation and Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Scale | Description |
|------------|--------|-------------|
| Quality Evaluation | 4 = Unacceptable, 3 = Poor, 2 = Good, 1 = Excellent | Assess life areas or solutions |
| Classification | Descending order of magnitude | Highlight critical issues |
| Selection (80/20 Rule) | Top 20% → 80% impact | Focus on leverage points |

## B.3 Prioritization Factors

| Symbol | Name | Levels | Meaning |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| PI | Positive Impact | — | Improvement magnitude |
| I | Interconnection | 1–Low, 2–Medium, 3–High | Systemic effect |
| T | Time Demand | 1–Low, 2–Medium, 3–High | Effort required |
