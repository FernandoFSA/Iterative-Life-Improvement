# Example PDCA Cycle

## PLAN
- Identified lack of physical activity affecting mood and focus.
- Root cause: poor time management and low motivation.
- Goal: Exercise 3x per week for 30 minutes.

## DO
- Scheduled workouts at 7:00 AM every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

## CHECK
- After 3 weeks: 80% completion rate, improved energy levels.

## ACT
- Corrective action: set reminders 15 minutes before sessions.
- Standardization: morning workouts become a daily routine.
